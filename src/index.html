ISLAND LIVING AT PALM JEBEL ALI

Luxurious 5,6,7 Bedroom Beach Villas, 80 New Hotels And 110 km Of Coastline!

Palm Jebel Ali is one of three ‘Palm Islands’ in Dubai. The one-of-a-kind community will be shaped like a palm tree, with a trunk and 17 branches. Over 35,000 households will be allowed to settle on the new island.

The luxurious, crescent-shaped island will have a first-class entertainment area. Once completed, the area will be one of the most popular tourist destinations in the Middle East, providing owners who rent out their property with a high income.

The developer is a well-known company in Dubai - Nakheel Properties. It adheres to Dubai Economic Agenda D33 and Dubai 2040 Urban Master Plan requirements. At Palm Jebel Ali, 30% of the public facilities will be powered by renewable energy.

Palm Jebel Ali Masterplan

In an announcement, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Vice-President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, revealed the forthcoming Palm Jebel Ali masterplan.

This visionary project is set to bring about an impressive array of developments, featuring a staggering 80 hotels and an astounding 110 kilometers of pristine coastline.

Palm Jebel Ali Location

A quick drive to other Dubai locations from Palm Jebel Ali is accessible via Sheikh Zayed Rd. Dubai International Airport is a 50-minute drive, and Al Maktoum International Airport is a 35-minute drive.

SIGHTS NEARBY INCLUDE:

  • The Walk JBR - a 35-minute drive
  • Dubai Mall - a 40-minute drive
